Biography

I am a visiting lecturer (Macao Fellow) at Faculty of Science and Technology in the University of Macau. My research interest includes text summarization, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020, supervised by Prof. Irwin King. In spring 2019, I worked with Prof. Lu Wang at Northeastern University as a visiting PhD student.
